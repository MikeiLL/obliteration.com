Went up to Natures Friends Farm in Pompton Lakes, NJ. It's an artist community on this old piece of sacred Native American land. Lot's of elder healers come through there, and they have a sweat lodge and stuff... These are people who live their lives based around the knowledge that spiritualism and naturalism are greater than materialism. Folks work hard, they hard core. I was talkin' to kids out there who like, live in the hills for weeks at a time, and deal with bears and shit... My man Nesta's like, (well, we we're talkin' about tryina find and keep love, and suicide (like, almost doing it) and how Bunny Wailer (of The Wailers) dissapears and lives in the bush in Jamaica and he's all alone, and nobody see's him for weeks and stuff) if you come accros a bear in the woods you'll smell him first, they smell really bad. And he's like, just stand there with your arms spread out, and don't run, so he knows you're a hunter too. That's some pain I can't even begin to imagine, and i doubt you can either. the wisdom in these eyes, as she tells me crying, that she just wants half-a-heart's worth of love.
