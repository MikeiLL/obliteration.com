03/20/00


Happy Spring

Wow. Has it really been 4 weeks since I last wrote? What a nutty time. Finally got the book/CD out hours before leaving on this 8 week tour. Was late for a show in Philly the first date and didn't get paid; it was still fun. Shows in Richmond and Ashville were very cool. Both great cities.

Today I'm in Huntsville, Alabama. This town doesn't have shit going on as far as an alternative scene. Played a blues jam last night in a coffee house. It's run by a very talented and cool guitarist named Jim Cavender. Everybody doing these fuckin' played out cover songs and I got kinda depressed. Went to sleep in the van at like 12:30 A.M. and dreamed about crying with my father over abortions he read about in my book. Don't know if he's read the stuff yet, but as far as I know he doesn't know... Do know that he thinks of it as loosing grandchildren.

At a Kinko's now. Been here for like 4 hours making phone calls and mailing shit out. Inside the van looks like a slight war zone... shit everywhere. I got to put sponsors logo.s up on the web site. Everything's always so chaotic and out of control. Must eat.

I'm so blessed to be able to travel and play. It's been rainy and I feel lonely in this town - military. Tried to call my love but our phone's disconnected. Do we have enough to pay the bill?

Stopped smoking. Ain't even smoked herb in almost a week. Last Tuesday got so stoned before performing in New York the show was fuckin' terrible. I'm lookin' at all my friends thinkin' "They're just paying dues, supporting."

Why is this country so conservative. People are so scared of the fuckin' truth. What's so wrong about sexuality? It is the cause of our existence.

Grey day.

Mike
